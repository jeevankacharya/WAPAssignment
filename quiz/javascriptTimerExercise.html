1. What is the difference between the following 2 statements?

setTimeout(booyah, 2000);

setTimeout(booyah(), 2000);

Ans : In setTimeout(booyah, 2000)  , function booyah is called after 2000 ms 

But in setTimeout(booyah(), 2000) , function booyah is called immediately neglating the timer.
